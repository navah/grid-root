welcome to navah.site :)

hi, my name is navah (NA-vah, rhymes with 'lava'). this website is a showcase (or storage space) for projects and activities.

i am particularly interested in numerical modeling and visualizing information.

my PhD research (University of Chicago, 2020) applied tried-and-true numerical methods to new questions at the intersection of climate sciences and seafloor geology. my other scientific exploits include mantle dynamics and evolutionary biology.

if you came here looking for JokesByDad, Ted Cruz fanfiction, Ben Bernenke fanfiction, or Climate Shawty, i'm sorry to report that those projects have been retired. however, i am still the climate shawty and am happy to help with any climate science questions.
